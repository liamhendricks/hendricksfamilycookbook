Fantastic salad dressing by Shayne Eliason. 

## Ingredients
* 1/2 cup vinegar (rice or balsamic)
* 1/4 oil (vegetable or olive)
* 1 TB brown sugar
* 1 TB honey
* 2 tsp Dijon mustard
* 1 cloves of garlic (finely minced)
* 1/4 tsp salt
* 1/4 tsp pepper
