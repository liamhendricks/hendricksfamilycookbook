Favorite punch for the Hendricks bunch. 

## Ingredients
* 2 cans lime frozen concentrate (no water added)
* 2 2-liter Diet Sprite 
* 1 cucumber, sliced
* Ice 

### Directions
1. Depending on size of container, add all ingredients (or half) and then add remaining as needed.
