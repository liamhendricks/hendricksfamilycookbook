Ingredients:

* 1﻿/2 cup sugar
* 1﻿ tsp. salt
* 6﻿ cups flour
* 1﻿ 1/2 cakes yeast
* 1﻿/4 cup shortening
* 2﻿ cups warm milk
* 2﻿ eggs

Directions:
