Ingredients

* 1﻿ cup flour
* 1﻿ cup water
* 1﻿ TB oil
* 1﻿/2 cup salt
* 1﻿ tsp cream of tartar
* Food coloring - any color

Directions

Cook over medium heat until mixture pulls away from sides of pan and becomes like playdough consistency. Knead until cool. Keeps 3 months unrefrigerated.
