Recipe from Jenny Sampson, a friend from the neighborhood. 

## Ingredients
* 1 1/2 cups butter
* 2 cups sugar
* 1/2 cup molasses
* 4 tsp soda
* 4 cups flour
* 2 tsp cinnamon
* 1/2 tsp salt
* 2-2 1/2 tsp ginger

### Directions
1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. 
2. Mix wet, then dry ingredients.
3. Chill dough.
4. Bake for 8-10 minutes.
