This is more like a dessert than cornbread! 

## Ingredients

* 2 eggs, beaten
* 1 cup milk
* 2 cups Bisquick mix
* 3/4 cup sugar
* 1/2 tsp baking soda
* 3 TB Cornmeal

### Directions
1. Preheat over to 350 degrees. 
2. Mix ingredients together. 
2. Bake in greased 9 x 13 pan for 30 minutes.
