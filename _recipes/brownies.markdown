Famous brownie recipe from the Thornock family. 

## Ingredients
* 1 1/2 cups sugar
* 2 tsp vanilla
* 1 tsp salt
* 1/2 cup Crisco
* 4 eggs
* 1/2 cup butter
* 1/2 cup cocoa
* 1/2 cup flour

### Frosting
* 1/4 cup butter
* 1/4 cup milk
* 1/8 tsp salt
* 3 TB cocoa

### Directions
1. Stir wet ingredients, followed by dry ingredients. 
2. Bake at 350-365 degrees for 28 minutes.
